What is DivZero

DivZero is a community for young Korean and Korean American professionals committed to exceptional growth — independent thinkers who expect more from themselves and one another. Through private discussions, panels, and events focused on technology, startups, and emerging ideas, we cultivate a network whose ambition and influence compound over decades.

Like division by zero, we aim toward infinity.

Our Story

DivZero began with The Almanack of Naval Ravikant and Paul Graham’s Cities and Ambition. Naval argues that your city, career, and partner shape who you meet and what you believe is possible. Graham shows how cities reinforce that effect: New York prizes wealth, Boston intellect, and Silicon Valley power. Every environment attracts and amplifies a particular kind of ambition — and choosing yours deliberately is one of the highest-leverage decisions you can make.
